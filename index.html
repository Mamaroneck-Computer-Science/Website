Why This Hackathon?

This is our school's first hackathon, designed to be welcoming and ambitious at the same time. You'll team up, build a project, and present it to judges from tech and education backgrounds.

Whether you code, design, pitch, or brainstorm ideas, there is a place for you here.

What makes this legit

  • Structured schedule with checkpoints
  • On-site mentor support
  • Published judging rubric
  • Clear safety and conduct policy

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need coding experience?

No. Beginners are welcome, and workshops are built for first-timers.

Can I participate without a team?

Yes. We run a team-matching session during check-in.

What should I bring?

Your laptop, charger, student ID, and curiosity.
